How it works

The MedBridge Journey

1. Secure Upload

Patients instantly upload raw, fragmented medical PDFs and clinical notes into their highly secure, encrypted personal dashboard via the Streamlit interface.

2. AI Extraction & Chunking

Under the hood, BioBERT processes complex terminology, chunks the text, and maps it directly into a local vector-space powered by ChromaDB.

3. Conversational RAG

Patients can immediately interact with our LLaMA-based AI Health Assistant, asking pinpoint questions about their deep medical history and receiving cited answers.

4. Instant Clinical Sync

Via cryptographic linking, doctors enter a shared access code and seamlessly ingest the mapped records directly into their own clinical panel view instantly.
